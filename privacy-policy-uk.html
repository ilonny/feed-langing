The Privacy Policy

This policy was last updated on 28.04.2020.

Feed Application Limited (“Feed”, “we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains the way in which we collect, manage, use and protect any personal data we obtain through your engagement with us, as well as your legal rights in respect of your data. By “personal data” we mean any information, alone or jointly with other similar information, that could be used to identify you.

This Policy covers any personal data we collect in connection with your use of the FEED app (the “App”) and www.feedapp.me (the “Website”), where you can learn more about the App and any other services we offer you (the “Feed Services”).

Use of our App and Website are subject to your agreement to the terms of this Privacy Policy. Our Terms of Use, Terms and Conditions and any other agreement we might have with you (as may be amended from time to time).

What personal data do we collect about you?

When you access the App or Website, we may collect a variety of personal data about you, including:

Data that you provide to us directly

Feed primarily collects personal data provided by you directly when you sign up to or use the Appor Website, including:

  • Name
  • Date of birth
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Your preferred language for use of the App
  • Any personal information contained within any user content you upload to the App (e.g. username, account password, photos, videos, audio recordings, any personal information you disclose about yourself in your user profile or interactions with other users)
  • Any personal information contained within any content on your device to which you have permitted App access (e.g. any data imported from your device with your permission such as images uploaded from your device photo gallery or address book for syncing contacts on the App)
  • Any information relating to your interactions with other users and accounts of the App, e.g. which groups you are part of, how you respond to other users’ content on the App, and which users you have added to your “favourites” list.
  • (For both the App and Website) Any information you choose to provide when you contact us with any questions or request our services (e.g. contact details, username, customer reference number).

We will let you know at the point of collecting your information whether this is optional, or whether it is necessary for you to provide this information to meet certain statutory or contractual requirements (e.g. to set up your user account). If the latter and you do not wish to provide us with this information, this may limit your ability to use the App or Website and the services we are able to provide to you.

Generally speaking, any content that might be uploaded to the App or sent to other users once you have a user account (e.g. video messages, profile information and photo) will be entirely optional and only done when you choose to do so.

How do we use your personal data and on what grounds?

We will only use your personal data if we have a permitted lawful basis to do so. The primary purpose for which we collect information about is to provide you with the services you have requested of us from the App and Website.

You don’t transfer any ownership of your data (or any social media pages you connect to the App) to us by using the App or Website - we only collect, store, and process data for specific purposes listed below.

To perform our contract with you (i.e. to facilitate your use of the App and Website)

  • To enable your use of the App, e.g. to create, manage and save your user account and preferences, join a feed and interact with other users, ensure cross-platform compatibility of the App with any other social media platforms you have connected to your account)
  • To provide you with information, products or services that you request from us
  • For handling contacts, queries, complaints or disputes

For our legitimate interests

  • Product and market research and analytics to understand user preferences for the App, improve the Feed Services and develop new products and services (generally this will be on an aggregated and anonymized basis)
  • To ensure network and information security in the Feed Services and your personal data
  • Protecting Feed and our users by taking appropriate legal action against third parties who have committed criminal acts or are in breach of legal obligations to us, or to enforce our contractual rights (e.g. investigating any suspected breaches of our terms of use)
  • To effectively handle any legal claims or regulatory enforcement actions taken against Feed
  • To generally run the App and Website and for internal operations, in order to provide you with an up to date, efficient and reliable service
  • Making important service communications about the App and Website (e.g. notifying you of any changes to terms and conditions)

To comply with our legal obligations

  • To comply with our legal and regulatory obligations (including under applicable data protection laws)
  • For preventing, investigating and detecting crime, fraud or anti-social behavior and prosecuting offenders, including working with law enforcement agencies
  • To fulfil our duties to our employees and other stakeholders

With your consent

  • To keep you updated with the latest developments and offers on the App
  • To send you push notifications on your device (e.g. when you receive a new comment on your profile or message)
  • Using your content in Feed’s promotional and marketing materials

We will only use your personal data for the purposes under “with your consent” where you have opted-in to such use (e.g. by enabling push notifications from the App when prompted on your device).

Where we rely on consent to process your personal data, you have the right to withdraw your consent to these activities at any time, which will mean (unless another lawful basis applies to your data) that we will cease to process the affected data after consent is withdrawn. However, please note this may result in us being unable to provide you with certain features of the Feed Services.

If you wish to withdraw your consent to any of the above activities, you can do so by adjusting your preferences on your user account or contacting us at info@feedapp.me.

Will my personal data be sent abroad?

All personal data processed in connection with the App and the Website is stored securely on our on-premises servers in the United States. We rent those servers from Google Cloud as our hosting partner. Data is backed up to a regional datacenter within the EU as well via ​Google Cloud Platform​ and ​Amazon Web Services​.

If you are based in the UK or EU this means we need to transfer your personal data in the outside the European Economic Area, and additional regulations may apply under applicable data protection laws to ensure a similar degree of protection is afforded to your personal data. Such additional regulations may also apply to any future transfers of personal data outside the EEA.

At the date of this policy the position in respect of transfers of data between the UK and EU are constantly changing, but we will ensure that such transfers are made subject to appropriate safeguards as may be required by any applicable data protection laws from time to time. These will include ensuring that the data is only located in a country deemed to hold adequacy status or through the use of approved standard contractual clauses.

For more details on the safeguards in place for transfers of your personal data abroad, including how to obtain copies of these where relevant, please contact us using the details below.

How long will you hold my data?

We keep your data for as long as it’s necessary to meet the relevant purposes for which we’ve collected your data, including for the purpose of satisfying any legal, accounting or reporting requirements.

Generally speaking we will retain your personal data as long as you keep your account open or as needed to provide you the Feed Services, including data you or others provided to us from your use of the Feed Services. Once your account is cancelled and deactivated, or if you have made a formal request for the deletion of your personal data in accordance with the section “Your rights over your personal data” below, all your personal will be permanently removed or anonymized.

However, please note that permanent deletion may not always be possible based on the nature of your data. For example, content you have shared with other users or if you participate in a public online conference “room”, will mean that your audio/video messages will continue to exist as part of a larger feed with content from other users on the other participants’ profiles and can’t be deleted unless all other relevant users also delete their accounts.

In some instances we may also need to hold your personal data beyond the duration of your account, for example if you are still signed up to receive marketing newsletters from us (until you withdraw your consent) or to comply with any legal record-keeping obligations. In particular, we will retain product usage and customer support logs for up to 4 weeks after your account cancellation/data deletion request, and will request that our service providers remove that data from their servers as well as removing it ourselves after that. Where possible, we will take extra steps to protect your personal data if this needs to be retained longer than the retention periods specified here, such as anonymization.

To determine the appropriate length of time for holding your data, we consider the amount, nature, and sensitivity of the personal data, the potential risk of harm, from unauthorized use or disclosure of your personal data, the purpose for which we process your data and whether we can achieve those purposes through other means, along with the applicable legal requirements.

How do you keep my personal data safe?

We have taken appropriate security measures to protect your personal data from destruction, loss, unauthorized provision or other unauthorized operation. In particular, to the extent appropriate and possible we rely on the use of cryptographic techniques, logging, adoption of pseudonymization of data, as well as ensuring the permanent confidentiality, integrity and resilience of processing systems and regular testing, assessment and evaluation of the effectiveness of technical and organizational measures to ensure safety of your data.

Your rights over your personal data

If you are based in the UK or EU, you may, in circumstances, have the following rights in relation to the Personal Data we hold about you.

You can exercise all the rights applicable to you by contacting us via email at support@feedapp.me.

You can request to:

  • access a copy of the information held about you. You can request a copy of all personal data that we hold about you. We do not remove your data when you request a download.
  • rectify any incorrect or incomplete data we hold about you. It is in both our interests that any personal information we hold about you is accurate, complete and current. If the data we hold about you is inaccurate in any way, please contact us to have your personal information corrected. You can also update any incorrect contact information yourself by updating your user profile information on the App.
  • delete, restrict or remove the data we hold about you. You can request to temporarily suspend your account or delete it and all personal data we hold about you, to the extent possible, where you believe our processing of your personal data is no longer necessary for the purpose for which it was obtained or you have withdrawn your consent for such processing. Please see the section “How long will you hold my data?” above for further details about how we deal with any data removal requests.
  • transfer the data we hold about you to another party, subject to any technical system constraints between the parties
  • object to any further processing of your data, if we are processing your personal data on the basis of our legitimate interests (see “How do we use your personal data and for what grounds” above), or for direct marketing purposes

We will endeavor to respond to your requests within one month and free of charge. Please note that in respect of all these rights, we reserve the right to:

  • refuse your request based on the exemptions set out in the applicable data protection legislation
  • request for proof of your ID to process the request or request further information
  • charge you a reasonable administrative fee for any repetitive, manifestly unfounded or excessive requests

If we refuse your request to exercise these rights, we will give reasons for our refusal and allow you to challenge our decision. If we have shared your data with others, we will tell them about your request to rectify, erase, restrict or object to the processing where possible. If you ask us, where possible and lawful to do so, we will also tell you with whom we shared your personal data so that you can contact them directly.

If you have any concerns about how we handle your data, please contact us. If you are not satisfied after we’ve tried to resolve your issue, you’ll be entitled to lodge a complaint with the data protection regulator in your relevant jurisdiction. If you are based in the UK, this is the Information Commissioners’ Office. If you are based in the EU, you can find your relevant local supervisory authority here: https://edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/board/members_en.
