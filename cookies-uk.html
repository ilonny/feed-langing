What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that identify your browser or device. Cookies are stored on your browser when you visit the Website and stored on your device (computer or other device with Internet access, such as a smartphone or tablet). Cookies usually contain the name of the website from which they originate, validity, login and value.

The next time you visit the Website, the web browser will recognize the cookies and your browsing device and send this information back to the Website which originally created the cookie. Cookies we use do not harm your computer.

Cookies can remain on your device for different periods of time. Some cookies are 'session cookies', which means they exist only while your browser is open, and they are deleted automatically once you close your browser. Other cookies are 'permanent cookies', meaning that they survive after your browser is closed. They can be used by our Website to recognize your computer when you open your browser and browse the Internet again. Except for essential cookies, all cookies will expire after 2 years.

For more information what are the cookies and how they work, please visit the Information Commissioner’s Office website.
