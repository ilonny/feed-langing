Your rights

We do not remove your data when you request a download. If you wish to download your data, please contact our customer experience team: info@feedapp.me.

You may also request data erasure by removing your profile from the Service via email to info@feedapp.me. When you request erasure,

  • all your personal and social data will be removed from product infrastructureincluding our database,
  • the project data such as social media data, analytics data, publications plan,posts drafts will be removed from all our infrastructure,
  • we will retain product usage and customer support logs for up to 4 weeks afteryour account cancelation, and will request that our service providers remove that data from their servers as well as removing it ourselves after that.

However, your complete data deletion is not possible in case that you participate in a public “room” that means online conference room where you communicate with other users via video/audio messages”. Once you participate in such meeting with other users, the video becomes a part of other users’ profiles and can’t be deleted.
