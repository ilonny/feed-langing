What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that identify your browser or device. Cookies are stored on your browser when you visit an app or website and stored on your device (computer or other device with Internet access, such as a smartphone or tablet). Cookies usually contain the name of the website or app from which they originate, validity, login and value. The next time you visit the site or app the web browser or app recognize the cookies and your browsing device and sends this information back to the website or app that originally created the cookie. Cookies we use do not harm your computer. For more information what are the cookies and how they work, please visit Europa web Guide.

We use several types of cookies on our website

Basic cookies
These cookies form the basis for the operation of our app or website and allow the use of basic functions, such as secure areas or online payments. Basic cookies are e.g. remembering the login, enabling access to secure areas without the need to log in again, pre-filling the forms, etc. Without these cookies, we cannot provide the services that form the basis of our app or website. If you disable these cookies, we will not be able to guarantee the flawless operation of the app or website.
Operational cookies
We use operational cookies to collect statistical information about how you use our app or website. These are technical information, e.g. they will tell us which parts of the app or website you clicked on, which page you last visited, etc. Cookies are used by us to analyze and improve our app and website in terms of content, performance and design. If you disable these cookies, we cannot guarantee proper operation of our site.
Functional cookies
Functional cookies are not necessary, but they help us improve the functionality of our app and website. This is, for example, remembering the settings selected during a previous visit to the page, e.g. content layout, site selection, etc., so you do not have to set them up again. We use these cookies to determine if you have already been offered a particular service or provide information from your current area if you agree to share such information. Although the use of these cookies depends solely on your settings, if you turn them off, you could lose some of the services we could provide to you.
Advertising cookies
We may use advertising cookies on our app or website to display content and advertisements that are tailored to your interests. These cookies may collect information about your browsing habits on our website. They may also be used to recognize your revisits to any of the websites that are part of our network. In this way, we can provide content and advertisement on our website that are more relevant and effective for you, targeting direct marketing communications, but also limiting the number of ad impressions and measuring the effectiveness of your advertising campaigns.
Third party cookies
Feed contains links and integrated content from other websites. Therefore, cookies, which may be created during the use of the app or website, are not subject to control of Feed. This is the case, for example, if the viewed website or our app uses a tool for analysis or marketing automation from a third party (such as Google) or displays the content of third party websites, e.g. YouTube or Facebook. This results in the acceptance of cookies from these third party services. Feed cannot control the storage of or access to these cookies. To know how these third parties use cookies, read their privacy policy and the policies they use.
Cookies can remain on your device for different periods of time. Some cookies are 'session cookies', it means they exist only while your browser is open, and they are deleted automatically once you close your browser. Other cookies are 'permanent cookies', meaning that they survive after your browser is closed. They can be used by our app to recognize your computer when you open your browser and browse the Internet again.
